Change Password

In order to protect your account, make sure
your password:

  • Is not the same as your old password
  • Does not match or significantly contain your
    email or username, e.g do not use 'username123'.
  • has atleast 8 characters
  • has a mixture of letters and numbers
  • Inclusion of atleast one special character, e.g..!
    @ # ? | Note: do not use < or > in your password,
    as both can cause problems in Web browsers.
